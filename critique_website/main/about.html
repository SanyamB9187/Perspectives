ABOUT

The Critic Behind
the Curtain

This is a space for slow thinking in a fast world. A place where a film isn't just a rating, a book isn't just a recommendation, and a game isn't just a score.

I believe the best criticism is a form of storytelling itself — an invitation to see familiar things differently.

Whether it's an arthouse film that haunts you for weeks, a last-second play that defies logic, or a sentence so perfect it stops you mid-breath — I'm here to notice, to feel, and to write about it honestly.

"Criticism is the autobiography of the critic's mind."

— A. C. Bradley
